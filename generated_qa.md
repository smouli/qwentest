## SECTION 1.1 — AFFILIATE

Q1: What is the definition of "Affiliate" in this Agreement?
A1: An Affiliate refers to any entity that directly or indirectly Controls, is Controlled by, or is under common Control with another entity. Additionally, North Star Capital, Ltd. and its Affiliates are specifically considered Affiliates of BLUESTONE ADVISORS L.P. under this Agreement.

Q2: Are North Star Capital, Ltd. and its Affiliates considered Affiliates of BLUESTONE ADVISORS L.P.?
A2: Yes, North Star Capital, Ltd. and its Affiliates are deemed to be Affiliates of BLUESTONE ADVISORS L.P. for purposes of this Agreement.

Q3: How is the relationship of Control determined for Affiliate status?
A3: Control is determined by direct or indirect relationships where one entity Controls, is Controlled by, or is under common Control with another entity, as defined in Section 1.4.

## SECTION 1.2 — AGGREGATED DATA

Q1: What is "Aggregated Data"?
A1: Aggregated Data refers to any aggregated, de-identified data relating to the access or use of the Services by or on behalf of COMPANY, including any performance, analytics or statistical data that INTER INTEL may collect from time to time.

Q2: What types of data are included in Aggregated Data?
A2: Aggregated Data includes performance, analytics or statistical data that INTER INTEL may collect from time to time relating to the access or use of the Services by or on behalf of COMPANY.

Q3: Does Aggregated Data include personally identifiable information?
A3: No, Aggregated Data is specifically defined as de-identified data, meaning it does not contain personally identifiable information.

## SECTION 1.4 — CONTROL

Q1: How is "Control" defined in this Agreement?
A1: Control and its derivatives mean: (a) the legal, beneficial or equitable ownership, directly or indirectly, of at least fifty percent (50%) of the aggregate of all voting equity interests in an entity, or equity interests having the right to at least fifty percent (50%) of the profits of an entity or, in the event of dissolution, to at least fifty percent (50%) of the assets of an entity; (b) the right to appoint, directly or indirectly, a majority of the board of directors; (c) the right to control, directly or indirectly, the management or direction of the entity by contract or corporate governance document; or (d) in the case of a partnership, the holding by an entity (or one of its Affiliates) of the position of sole general partner.

Q2: What percentage of voting equity interests constitutes Control?
A2: At least fifty percent (50%) of the aggregate of all voting equity interests in an entity constitutes Control.

Q3: Can Control be established through the right to appoint directors?
A3: Yes, the right to appoint, directly or indirectly, a majority of the board of directors constitutes Control.
